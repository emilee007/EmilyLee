Hedwig and the Angry Inch

branding, interactive, motion

As an Interactive Designer at Situation Interactive, I designed e-mail, as well as storyboarded and developed flash banners for HEDWIG, the Broadway musical.

I enjoyed working on HEDWIG because its branding truly stands apart from other Broadway shows. I particularly liked working on the flash banners, which are animated in an exciting, dramatic way. I storyboarded and developed the Tony nomination banners and gave creative direction to a freelance flash developer for the Andrew Rannells banners.
