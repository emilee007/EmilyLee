The Metropolitan Opera

branding, interactive, motion

As an Interactive Designer at Situation Interactive, I designed landing pages and e-mail, as well as storyboarded and developed flash banners for The Metropolitan Opera. I enjoyed working on The Metropolitan brand because of its elegant, sophisticated image and the fact that I was able to work with many beautiful production photos. I particularly like the “At the Met” banners, which show the the experience of going to the Met through the panning of photos.
