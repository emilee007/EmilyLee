Rock of Ages

branding, interactive

As an Interactive Designer at Situation Interactive, I designed the website, e-mail, banners, and landing pages for the Broadway and Las Vegas productions of the ROCK OF AGES musical.

I enjoyed working on the ROCK OF AGES brand since I was able to create flashy, original art (including graffiti on a bathroom stall and neon signs) that complemented the show for the interior pages of the website and landing pages.
