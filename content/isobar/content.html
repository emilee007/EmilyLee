Isobar Run for the Wild

branding, illustration

As a Visual Designer at Isobar, I helped design t-shirts for the Isobar team participating in Run for the Wild. Run for the Wild is an annual 5K marathon at the Bronx Zoo supporting the Wildlife Conservation Society.

Aside from designing a few t-shirts while in grade school and college, this was my first foray into t-shirt design for work. I had a lot of fun since it's different from my usual projects, and was completely self-directed. I decided early on that I wanted to include an animal pun and sketched three additional ideas involving porpoises, lions, and koalas, before settling on this one.
