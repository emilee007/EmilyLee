Vintage Seltzer

art direction, branding, illustration, packaging

I rebranded Vintage Seltzer Water by giving it a more youthful look. The logotype is friendly and rounded, with bubbles overlaid in a playful manner. The illustrations on the cans appeals to a younger crowd, as a healthier alternative to soda. The new branding is meant to show that drinking Vintage Seltzer is refreshing, taking you on an underwater journey.

To distinguish between different flavors, each flavor is represented by different colors. The original features a treasure chest with bubbles, while the other flavors feature an open oyster shell with fruit in place of a pearl.
