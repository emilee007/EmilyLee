The Mysterious Bookshop

art direction, branding, illustration, interactive, motion, packaging, print

The Mysterious Bookshop is an independent bookstore for people who love mystery books. I developed their physical and online presence by creating a consistent identity for them. In rebranding The Mysterious Bookshop, I targeted mystery book aficionados of every age as well as new readers who have not yet have been introduced to the thrill of a good mystery. I emphasized that the bookshop is one-of-a-kind and gave customers a more interactive experience by playing on the idea of finding clues and solving mysteries.
