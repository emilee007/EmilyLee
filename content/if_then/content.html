If/Then

branding, interactive

As an Interactive Designer at Situation Interactive, I designed e-mail, banners, and social media assets (including contests and bumper images) for Broadway Musical If/Then.

Translating If/Then's print artwork to digital formats proved to be particularly challenging, as the artwork transitioned from being illustration-only to a marriage of illustration and photography.

One of the most interesting campaigns that I was able to work on was a full takeover of BroadwayWorld.com. It was a really cool experience designing each individual component, and being able to see them all come together live on the website.
